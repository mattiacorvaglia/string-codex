About

Welcome to this project — a simple and practical tool designed for musicians, students, producers, and music theory enthusiasts.

The goal of this website is to provide a fast and intuitive way to generate and explore the most important diatonic scales, including major, natural minor, harmonic minor, and melodic minor scales. Whether you are practicing your instrument, studying music theory, composing, improvising, or simply looking for a quick reference, this tool is built to make the process immediate and distraction-free.

Unlike complex music software filled with unnecessary features, this application focuses on clarity and usability. Just choose a root note and a scale type to instantly visualize the notes of the scale with correct musical notation, including sharps, flats, and advanced theoretical spellings when needed.

This project was developed by mcdev, with the idea of creating a lightweight and accessible reference tool that musicians can use every day — both for learning and for practical musical workflows.

Thanks for visiting, and enjoy exploring music theory.
