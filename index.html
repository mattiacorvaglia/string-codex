About

Welcome to String Codex — a simple and practical tool designed for musicians, students, producers, and music theory enthusiasts.

This project was developed by Mattia Corvaglia, with the idea of creating a lightweight and accessible reference tool that musicians can use every day — both for learning and for practical musical workflows.

Thanks for visiting, and enjoy exploring music theory.
